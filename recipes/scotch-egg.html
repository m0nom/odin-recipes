Scotch eggs

Ingredients

We need a few more ingredients (one from a previous recipe ;3)

  • 500 gr Ground beef
  • 4 boiled eggs
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Panko
  • Any neutral oil
  1. Mix the ground beef with your fav spices, or whatever you have laying around (I usually use minced garlic, black pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika, rosemary and salt)
  2. Knead the beef until it becomes dough-like
  3. Put a piece of plastic on the table
  4. Add a sheet of ground beef
  5. Lightly roll the egg in flour and put in the middle of the beef
  6. Carefully cover the egg in the meat using the plastic
    • You can do the motions like a candy wrapper
  7. Start heating up your oil, it should be around 160-180 degrees celsius.
  8. While the oil is heating up, grab one of your meat covered eggs, pass them thru eggs and then panko
  9. Fry until golden brown, or an internal temp of 65-70 degrees celsius
  10. You can also skip the oil to make this healthier and just put them in a baking pan and put them in the oven at 350F/180C, until golden brown
