Boiled Eggs

Boiled eggs

ingredients

We are just gonna need 2 ingredients!

  • Egg
  • Water

Directions

  1. Add a cm of water to pot
  2. As soon as it starts boiling add your eggs to bottom of the pot
  3. Get them out after the time has passed, it depends on the doneness you want:
    • 5 minutes soft boil
    • 6 minutes semisoft boil
    • 10 minutes hardboil
